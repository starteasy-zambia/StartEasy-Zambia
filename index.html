Our Services

Three integrated divisions

1) StartEasy Setup (Formalization)

Handles government & legal process friction.

  • PACRA registration
  • ZRA TPIN & VAT
  • NAPSA employer setup
  • Licenses & permits
  • ZDA guidance
  • Bank account assistance

2) StartEasy Activate (Digital & Business Systems)

  • Digital Presence: Website, domain, hosting, business email, Google Business Profile, online store
  • Payments & Tools: Mobile money (MTN, Airtel, Zamtel), POS guidance, invoices, booking systems
  • Operational Systems: Quotation templates, CRM, WhatsApp automation, inventory tracking, payroll guidance

3) StartEasy Represent (Ongoing Support)

  • Local representation & document collection
  • Property inspections
  • Permit renewals & compliance reminders
  • Follow-ups at offices
  • Contractor coordination
  • Statutory payments assistance & monthly reporting
